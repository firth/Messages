Status Message (April 10):

If you can see this message, you are on an old (broken) version of Weather Radar Pro.

To force the Windows Store to check for the update:

1. Open the Windows Store Metro App
2. Swipe in from the right side of the screen to open the Charms Bar. If you are using a mouse you can go to the hot corner at the top or bottom of the right side of your screen. You can also use the keyboard shortcut Windows C.
3. Tap or select the Settings menu item on the Charms Bar (bottom option).
4. Tap or select App Updates from the list of menu items on the Windows Store Settings Bar.
5. On the App Updates screen just tap or select Check for updates.
